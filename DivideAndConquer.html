Divide and Conquer

Overview

For the Divide and Conquer section of our comps, we developed a three-day teaching sequence introducing students to Merge Sort and Binary Search.

This unit teaches students how to break large problems into smaller subproblems, understand recursion, and analyze algorithmic assumptions about data.

Students worked through discussions, a coding assignment, slides, and reflections that connect efficiency, structure, and algorithmic impact.
